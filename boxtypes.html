Pawan Kalyan (born Konidela Kalyan Babu;[1] 2 September 1971[n 1]) is an Indian actor, filmmaker, philanthropist, and politician. His films are predominantly in Telugu cinema. Kalyan is the younger brother of actor-politician Chiranjeevi, and made his debut in the 1996 film Akkada Ammayi Ikkada Abbayi. He starred in Tholi Prema in 1998, which won the National Film Award for Best Feature Film in Telugu that year. He left the party after Chiranjeevi merged it into the Congress party.
